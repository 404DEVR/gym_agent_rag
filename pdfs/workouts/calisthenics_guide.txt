CALISTHENICS AND BODYWEIGHT TRAINING GUIDE

PROGRESSIVE CALISTHENICS PRINCIPLES
- Start with basic movements and progress to advanced variations
- Focus on proper form over quantity
- Use time under tension and tempo manipulation
- Progress by increasing reps, sets, or difficulty variations

UPPER BODY CALISTHENICS PROGRESSION

PUSH-UP VARIATIONS (Chest, Shoulders, Triceps):
Beginner:
- Wall Push-ups: 3 sets of 10-15 reps
- Incline Push-ups (using bed/chair): 3 sets of 8-12 reps
- Knee Push-ups: 3 sets of 8-12 reps

Intermediate:
- Standard Push-ups: 3 sets of 10-20 reps
- Wide-grip Push-ups: 3 sets of 8-15 reps
- Diamond Push-ups: 3 sets of 5-12 reps

Advanced:
- Decline Push-ups: 3 sets of 8-15 reps
- One-arm Push-ups: 3 sets of 3-8 reps per arm
- Handstand Push-ups: 3 sets of 5-10 reps

PULL-UP ALTERNATIVES (Back, Biceps):
No Bar Options:
- Towel Door Rows: 3 sets of 8-15 reps
- Reverse Plank: 3 sets of 30-60 seconds
- Superman: 3 sets of 15-20 reps

With Bar/Playground:
- Dead Hang: 3 sets of 20-60 seconds
- Negative Pull-ups: 3 sets of 3-8 reps
- Pull-ups: 3 sets of 5-15 reps

LOWER BODY CALISTHENICS

SQUAT VARIATIONS (Quads, Glutes):
Beginner:
- Bodyweight Squats: 3 sets of 15-25 reps
- Wall Sits: 3 sets of 30-60 seconds
- Chair-assisted Squats: 3 sets of 10-20 reps

Intermediate:
- Jump Squats: 3 sets of 10-20 reps
- Single-leg Squats (assisted): 3 sets of 5-10 per leg
- Cossack Squats: 3 sets of 8-12 per leg

Advanced:
- Pistol Squats: 3 sets of 5-10 per leg
- Shrimp Squats: 3 sets of 3-8 per leg
- Bulgarian Split Squats: 3 sets of 10-15 per leg

LUNGE VARIATIONS (Quads, Glutes, Hamstrings):
- Forward Lunges: 3 sets of 10-15 per leg
- Reverse Lunges: 3 sets of 10-15 per leg
- Walking Lunges: 3 sets of 20-30 steps
- Jump Lunges: 3 sets of 10-20 per leg

CORE AND FULL BODY

CORE EXERCISES:
- Plank: 3 sets of 30-120 seconds
- Side Plank: 3 sets of 20-60 seconds per side
- Mountain Climbers: 3 sets of 20-40 reps
- Bicycle Crunches: 3 sets of 20-40 reps
- Leg Raises: 3 sets of 10-20 reps

FULL BODY MOVEMENTS:
- Burpees: 3 sets of 5-15 reps
- Bear Crawl: 3 sets of 20-40 steps
- Crab Walk: 3 sets of 20-40 steps
- Turkish Get-ups (bodyweight): 3 sets of 5 per side

WORKOUT ROUTINES

BEGINNER FULL BODY (3x/week):
1. Wall/Incline Push-ups: 3x10-15
2. Bodyweight Squats: 3x15-20
3. Plank: 3x30-45 seconds
4. Forward Lunges: 3x10 per leg
5. Superman: 3x15
6. Wall Sits: 3x30 seconds

INTERMEDIATE UPPER/LOWER SPLIT (4x/week):
Upper Day:
1. Push-ups: 3x10-15
2. Pike Push-ups: 3x8-12
3. Towel Door Rows: 3x10-15
4. Diamond Push-ups: 3x5-10
5. Plank: 3x45-60 seconds

Lower Day:
1. Jump Squats: 3x15-20
2. Bulgarian Split Squats: 3x10 per leg
3. Single-leg Glute Bridges: 3x12 per leg
4. Calf Raises: 3x20-25
5. Wall Sits: 3x45-60 seconds

ADVANCED FULL BODY (5-6x/week):
1. Handstand Push-ups: 3x5-10
2. Pistol Squats: 3x5-8 per leg
3. One-arm Push-ups: 3x3-5 per arm
4. Archer Pull-ups: 3x5-8 per arm
5. L-sit: 3x20-45 seconds
6. Burpees: 3x10-15

EQUIPMENT-FREE CARDIO:
- High Knees: 30-60 seconds
- Jumping Jacks: 30-60 seconds
- Burpees: 10-20 reps
- Mountain Climbers: 30-60 seconds
- Sprint in Place: 30-60 seconds

PROGRESSION TIPS:
- Increase reps by 1-2 each week
- Add 5-10 seconds to holds weekly
- Progress to harder variations when you can complete all sets with good form
- Rest 48-72 hours between training same muscle groups
- Focus on controlled movements and full range of motion
